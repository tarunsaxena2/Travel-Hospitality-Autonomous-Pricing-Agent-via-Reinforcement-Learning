# Project Context

Domain: Travel & Hospitality
Goal: Train a DQN agent that outperforms heuristic pricing baselines 
(fixed, time-based discount, demand-based) in mean episodic revenue 
across 1,000 simulated seasons.

Team: 4 members (Environment, RL Algorithm, Analysis, Eval & Deploy)
Duration: 4-week sprint
