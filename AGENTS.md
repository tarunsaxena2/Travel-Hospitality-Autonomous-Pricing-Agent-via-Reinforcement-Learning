# Team Agent Roles

## Member 1 — Environment & Simulation Engineer
Responsible for building and maintaining the Gymnasium environment: 
state/action spaces, demand simulation, reset/step logic, and stability testing.

## Member 2 — RL Algorithm Engineer
Responsible for reward function design, Q-Learning implementation, 
DQN architecture, and training loops.

## Member 3 — Analysis & Policy Evaluation
Responsible for evaluating agents, running experiments, plotting results, 
and analyzing learned policy behavior.

## Member 4 — Eval & Deploy Lead
Responsible for repo setup, GitHub issue/Kanban management, documentation, 
PR reviews, and final dashboard/deployment readiness.
